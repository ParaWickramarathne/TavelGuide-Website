Discover the Enchanting Beauty of Sri Lanka.

Welcome to Sri Lanka

 

Welcome to the mesmerizing island nation of Sri Lanka, where ancient history meets breathtaking landscapes, and vibrant cultures blend seamlessly. This travel guide is your key to unlocking the secrets of this jewel of the Indian Ocean. Whether you're an avid adventurer, a history enthusiast, or simply seeking a tranquil escape, Sri Lanka offers a diverse range of experiences that will leave you captivated.

Popular Destinations

Colombo

The vibrant capital city is a bustling metropolis where modernity meets tradition. Explore historical sites, vibrant markets, and enjoy the diverse culinary scene.

Kandy

Discover the cultural heart of Sri Lanka, home to the sacred Temple of the Tooth and surrounded by picturesque hills.

Sigiriya

Climb the iconic Sigiriya Rock Fortress for panoramic views of the surrounding landscape and explore the ancient frescoes that adorn its walls.

Galle

Wander through the charming streets of this UNESCO World Heritage Site, featuring a well-preserved Dutch fort and a unique blend of colonial architecture

Nuwara Eliya

Nuwara Eliya, with its cool climate and breathtaking landscapes, invites visitors to experience a different facet of Sri Lanka

Anuradhapura & Polonnaruwa

Anuradhapura and Polonnaruwa are both important ancient cities with hundreds of crumbling monuments

Natural Wonders

Ella

Escape to the cool hill country and witness the breathtaking Ella Gap, a lush valley surrounded by misty mountains. Don't miss the iconic Nine Arch Bridge.

Yala National Park

Embark on a safari adventure in one of the country's premier wildlife sanctuaries, known for its diverse flora and fauna, including leopards and elephants.

Mirissa

Relax on pristine beaches, go whale watching, and experience the laid-back vibe of this coastal paradise

Featured Hotels

Amanwella - Tangalle

Nestled along the golden shores of Tangalle, Amanwella offers a luxurious retreat with contemporary design, private villas, and stunning ocean views. With an emphasis on tranquility and personalized service, this boutique resort is a haven for those seeking a peaceful escape.

Ceylon Tea Trails - Hatton

Set amidst the scenic tea plantations in Hatton, Ceylon Tea Trails is a collection of luxurious bungalows that exude old-world charm. Guests can enjoy the elegance of colonial-style accommodations, personalized butler service, and breathtaking views of the surrounding hills.

Amangalla - Galle

Located within the historic Galle Fort, Amangalla is a heritage hotel that seamlessly blends colonial architecture with modern luxury. The property exudes a timeless charm, offering spacious suites, a serene courtyard, and a spa for a truly indulgent experience.

Ulagalla Resort - Anuradhapura

Situated amidst 58 acres of lush greenery near Anuradhapura, Ulagalla Resort provides a unique blend of luxury and eco-friendliness. The resort features private villas with plunge pools, a spa, and horse riding facilities, allowing guests to immerse themselves in nature and history.

Shangri-La Hotel - Colombo

In the heart of Colombo, the Shangri-La Hotel stands as a symbol of modern luxury. Overlooking the Indian Ocean, it offers contemporary rooms, diverse dining options, a rooftop bar, and a spa. The central location provides easy access to Colombo's vibrant attractions.

Cape Weligama - Weligama

Perched on a cliff in Weligama, Cape Weligama offers panoramic views of the Indian Ocean. The resort features spacious villas with private plunge pools, an infinity pool, and a range of dining options. It's an ideal destination for those seeking both luxury and authenticity.

Featured Activities

Climb Adam's Peak (Sri Pada)

Undertake the pilgrimage hike to the summit of Adam's Peak, also known as Sri Pada. The climb is particularly popular during the pilgrimage season, offering breathtaking views at sunrise.

Sigiriya Rock Climb

Embark on an exhilarating climb to the top of Sigiriya Rock, an ancient rock fortress with panoramic views of the surrounding landscape. Marvel at the well-preserved frescoes and the impressive Lion's Gate during this iconic adventure.

Hike to World's End in Horton Plains

Embark on a trek to World's End, a stunning escarpment in Horton Plains National Park. Enjoy breathtaking views of the surrounding hills and tea plantations from this elevated vantage point.

Temple of the Tooth Ceremony in Kandy

Witness the vibrant and spiritual atmosphere during the traditional Puja ceremonies at the Temple of the Tooth in Kandy. This sacred site houses the relic of the Buddha's tooth and is central to Sri Lanka's cultural heritage.

Cultural Show in Colombo

Attend a cultural show in Colombo to experience traditional dance, music, and performances. These shows often showcase the rich diversity of Sri Lanka's cultural heritage.

Whale Watching in Mirissa

Head to Mirissa, one of the best whale-watching destinations in the world. Take a boat excursion to witness majestic blue whales, sperm whales, and dolphins frolicking in the Indian Ocean.
